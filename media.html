A Letter to Jay

Dear me,

As Michelle Obama has shown through her journey, I too can rise above challenges, break barriers, and carve my own path. She once said, "Your story is what you have, what you will always have. It is something to own." Just as she embraced her story with confidence, I will embrace mine—fully, unapologetically, and with purpose.

She turned obstacles into stepping stones, proving that resilience and hard work can open doors once thought impossible. If she could use her voice to inspire change, I too can use my skills and passion to make an impact. Whether in technology, design, or the challenges ahead, I have the power to create, to lead, and to inspire others.

Like Michelle, I refuse to be limited by expectations. I will keep learning, keep pushing, and keep striving for excellence—because I am capable, I am strong, and I am becoming everything I am meant to be.
